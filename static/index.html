About ECOSIUM EVENT

Our mission

Our adventure began modestly with a few outdoor activities. Faced with the enthusiasm and positive feedback from our first clients, we gradually expanded our offerings. Today, ECOSIUM EVENT is proud to offer a full range of activities and workshops that meet the diverse needs of businesses and groups, while maintaining our commitment to the environment.

ECOSIUM EVENT offers tailor-made team building activities, adapted to your needs and objectives, which limit ENVIRONMENTAL impact.

Our Typical Program

A full day of activities to strengthen your team

8:30 AM - 9:00 AM

Participant Welcome

Warm welcome for participants at the farm. Icebreaker game to foster friendliness and break down barriers.

9:00 AM - 9:45 AM

Discovery Hike

A 45-minute hike to allow participants to discover the wonders of the farm and appreciate nature.

9:45 AM - 10:30 AM

Program Presentation

Gathering of all participants, presentation of the day's program, and team formation.

10:30 AM - 12:00 PM

Team Challenges

Various challenges: tug of war, archery, blindfolded football, and other team games to promote cohesion.

12:00 PM - 1:30 PM

Outdoor Lunch

Enjoy a friendly moment around an outdoor lunch, prepared by a quality caterer.

1:30 PM - 3:30 PM

3:30 PM - 4:30 PM

Communication and Leadership Session

A session dedicated to improving communication and leadership skills with practical exercises.

4:30 PM - 5:00 PM

End of Day and Debriefing

Conclusion of the day with a recap of highlights and sharing of participants' experiences and reflections.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions

What is the minimum group size?

We welcome groups starting from 10 people. For smaller groups, please contact us directly to discuss available options.

How can we customize our program?

We organize a preliminary meeting (in person or via Zoom/Google Meet) to understand your specific needs and adapt the program accordingly.

What are the payment terms?

A deposit of 30% of the total amount is required upon booking. The balance can be paid on the day of the event or by prior bank transfer.

What happens in case of bad weather?

We have covered areas for most activities. In case of extreme weather conditions, we can postpone the event to a later date.
